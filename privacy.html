YouTube Scheduler - Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 3, 2025

1. Information We Collect

When you use our app, we collect: Google account email, YouTube channel name, OAuth access tokens, and video metadata (title, description, hashtags).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to authenticate your account, schedule videos, and upload them to YouTube at the specified time.

3. Data Sharing

We only share your data with YouTube/Google (to upload videos) and our hosting providers. We do NOT sell your personal data to third parties.

4. Data Security

Your data is protected with HTTPS encryption, secure OAuth token storage, and regular security monitoring.

5. Your Rights

You can access your data, request corrections, delete your account, or revoke app permissions at any time.

6. YouTube API Usage

This app uses YouTube Data API v3. By using this app, you also agree to YouTube Terms of Service and Google Privacy Policy.

7. Contact

For privacy questions, contact: privacy@youtubescheduler.app
